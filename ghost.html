Ghost

Ghost was an individual with an eye for fashion. However, when he remembered his previous life, everything was thrown upside down and he fled the city and now lives among the ruins of the old cities.

A bit on the selfish side, he's often not well liked by the others hiding in the runs, as they find him too close to the enemies they are fighting.
