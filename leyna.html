Leyna

The current leader of Fortitude. While she's a level-headed leader, she's often spoke of as being too young. Only in her early twenties, she manages the entirety of the city and knows each resident by name.

She's extremely kind and empathic, but not so much as to ruin her judgement on the best procedures to keep the city safe. She makes it part of her daily habit to say hi to anyone she meets by name.
