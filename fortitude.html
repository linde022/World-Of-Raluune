Fortitude, the city in the center of Raluune. A stronghold in the sands left by a previous era. People in the wasteland often struggle for survival with the lack of water and the monsters roaming above, on, and below the sands. However. Fortitude is a city built of stone buildings and focused on sharing and trade and communication. People here band together and form strong bonds. Even the Drace, a commonly neglected race, find peace and happiness here.

This city was formed by those who fled Neo Eclipse as soon as the corruption started and they took their chance with the unforgiving world beyond the corporate walls. Among the more natural lifestyle of these people, magic has found some usage with the Drace teaching what they know.
