One of the harshest areas on Raluune, this place is deemed unfit for human life with the increasing amount of monsters and strang beings. Yet, feral Drace, those who reject their heritage and choose to live a wild life, tend to be found here. Many reduced to oppurtunistic hunters, but still a threat as much as the rest of the wildlife here.

However, the increasing number of monsters has led many to believe this to be the birthplace of these terrors, yet no human who has ventured here has come back, and Drace seem to refuse to speak of anything they've seen.
