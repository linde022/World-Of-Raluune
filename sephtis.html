Sephtis

Just a general nobody in Fortitude. Or that's what he leads everyone to believe. He's from a hidden facility in the Greater Wastes, and is a creature born from the void, an alternative dimension created from the Drace effectively splitting the harm to come to the world into it's own universe.

He was pulled here against his will and simply wants to live a normal life, though not being suited for life on the current Raluune, he is prone to fits.
