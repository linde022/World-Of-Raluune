The most populous city in Raluune, Neo Eclipse is a city embracing technology at it's core. Neon lights dot the skyscrapers and the rainy streets below. Surrounded by a large wall, this city is a stand alone government of it's own. People here can choose to live a comfortable life with technology catering to them and enough entertainment to never be bored.

Or... that's what people first entering the city are led to believe. The reality is much grimmer. The extreme class divide in the city leads to massive amounts of poor people and it's nearly impossible to climb the ladder unless you have connections or were born into the right family. Crime runs rampant as the payoff is worth the risk, despite many who participate hating the idea of the destruction and death they cause.
