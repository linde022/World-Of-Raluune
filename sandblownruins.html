Unlike the Lost Cities, these ruins have seen many years falling apart. These are believed to be the ruins of a previous civilization. One that doomed the world to it's current state. Many archeologists from Neo Eclipse flock here for a glimpse as to how to fix the world.

However, the Drace returning to the world and remembering their past insist to leave the past in the past. They refuse to talk about what they've lived through, further leading humans to be suspicious to them.
