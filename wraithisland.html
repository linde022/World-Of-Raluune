An island untouched by the disaster of the past. But also an island unknown to exist to many. This island is magical in nature, leading those to search for it to go lost and end up going around it. The only race known to frequent this island are the Drace, but even some of them aren't allowed within it's rainforest.

The reason this island remained untouched was that when the Drace sacrificed themselves to save the world, this island and the small area where Neo Eclipse and the Lost Cities are located were the only areas their combined power could protect from the corrosion.
