Arch

Arch was one of the oldest Drace, and considered their leader in her past life. She was the one to iniate the sacrifice of the Drace race to save the world. As such, she has been viewed in high respect from other reborn Drace. However, in her current life, she has a calling to the past, but never understood why until she regained her memories. Despite this, she still is working to recover the cracks in her memory and find a way for Drace and humans to coexist peacefully and prevent another disaster.

Arch as a Drace was noble and kind, and always looking out for others with no regard to herself. As such, she was viewed by both humans and other Drace as akin to a god. In her current life, even after remembering her past, she still retains a selfish streak, mostly when it comes to her work and credits, but she is rather selfless on all other accounts.
