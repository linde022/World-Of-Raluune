Meyna

Public enemy number two, behind Helios. But, no one knows who he is except his little activist group. Most know him by Digital Shadow, the activist responsible for multiple hacked airings to spread the word about the corruption of Neo Eclipse.

Highly destructive. Meyna always needs to get hands on when on missions, and leaves things in complete disarray. Despite this, he's a chill guy who tries to make friends with anyone, but not without a prank or two mixed in.
