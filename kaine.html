Kaine

Kaine is considered to be the only Drace alive who has seen the old civilization without fragmented memories. Likely because he never sacrificed himself. He was chosen to remain behind to help remind other Drace should a calamity come near again.

Generally a hermit, he doesn't interact with people or Drace often, and comes off as cold or just plain tired.
