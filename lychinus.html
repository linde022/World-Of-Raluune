Lychinus

A Drace born for the wild life, she was born in the desert as a first life Drace. She eventually found her way onto Wraith Island, where she remains with her partner Terron.

While she has some sense to her and can hold a conversation, she is very much wild and a cruel Drace, but won't kill sentient creatures without reason.
