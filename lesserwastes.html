This region is notable for it's scattered settlements. With the only way to reach this part of the world being to travel through the Greater Wastes or to travel the sea, people who make it here rarely see external threats. Even the monsters seem to avoid this area.

It is common to see caravans traveling through this region, both traveling groups, or those moving supplies. However, it seems an internal threat may affect the balance of these isolated cultures and maybe the world.
