Forgotten Island

The island the Drace once called home is now a sanctuary for wildlife the rest of the world has never seen. But amongst this are the Drace who watch over the world still. The Drace who don't intermingle with humanss.

They are the ones responsible for the inaccessibility of the island.

Many books from the previous civilization are kept on this island.
Shells are an important part of Drace culture.
Drace also practice natural healing with crystals and other elements.
