Underground

Crime runs rampant underground. Organized crime finds a home here, as well as the growing revolution, who teem up with the criminals in desperate times, to retake the city. A common sport, though inhumane, is pit fighting, commonly putting two trained Drace against each other in a fight to the death or at least to maim the opponent.

Credits are traceable so unbranded gold coins became the new currency.
Both personal injury and Drace fights are common forms of death, due to lack of medical care.
Many people flock to place bets on the top Drace fighters.
