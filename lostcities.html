These ruined cities were the last stand of the people fighting back against the corruption of Neo Eclipse. However, their defenses were unprepared for the Northern War, which left the cities as they are now. The citizens were assimilated back into Neo Eclipse when the war ended.

However, it seems people still live in these cities. It's unknown if they are people who refused to leave, or those that fled Neo Eclipse, or even from a different place. Regardless, they hold hatred for Neo Eclipse, but are hopeless to fight back after the decimation.
