Terron

Absolutely violent Drace. Born wild on Wraith Island by two Drace who abandoned his egg and left him to fend for himself. He grew up with the motto kill or be killed.

Cannot communicate through words, only through grunts and growls. He's not completely feral but his violent nature makes it seem like he is. Will kill anything that moves unless it can best him, similar to what happened with Lychinus, his mate.
